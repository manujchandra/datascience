Productivity Scripts :

I often find myself doing repetitive tasks on my computer. They may be related to my personal productivity system or official work.

One of the great things about the Linux operating system is that it offers an amazing amount of customization. Over a period of time I wrote some scripts to automate these tasks. You can find them here on my GitHub page.

Hopefully they will inspire you to create your own scripts to automate your workflow. Maybe you can use some scripts I have provided as it is or you may find bits and pieces and use them to put together your own system.

I also use AutoKey to automate repetitive commands. The key is to observe which tasks are consuming your time and then find ways to automate them.

Enjoy and stay productive!
